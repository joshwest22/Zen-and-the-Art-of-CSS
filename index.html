Discuss what makes Zen Garden interesting.

Zen Garden is not just your average website. It is special because it allows you to change stylesheets for the whole site. It is meant to encourage people to try their hand at CSS by developing a stylesheet for the site. They go as far as providing the html and stylesheet of their site for developers to base their designs on.
